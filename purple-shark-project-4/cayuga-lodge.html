Cayuga Lodge Cooperative

Cayuga Lodge is located on West Campus at 630 Stewart Ave.

Cayuga Lodge is the only co-op not owned by Cornell, but is still very involved with the cooperative community. Originally built 1880, the house became one of the first cooperative living institutions in the United States. It can house 18-22 residents, and is a very social place, hosting events for both lodgers and friends.

For more information, please email cayuga.lodge@gmail.com.
