Von Cramm Hall Cooperative

Von Cramm Hall is located on West Campus at 623 University Avenue.

Von Cramm Hall can house up to 33 residents, and is the largest cooperative house on campus. The residents are a mix of both undergraduate and graduate students, many of which are international students. The officers are elected by the the residents of the house, and there are also house meetings where members are able to vote on certain house policies, how to maintain the house, and how to manage the budget. Residents can become closer to each other with social gatherings, game nights, and pool tournaments.

For more information, please email voncrammcoop@cornell.edu.
