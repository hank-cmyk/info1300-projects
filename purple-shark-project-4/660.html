660 Stewart Cooperative

660 is located on West Campus at 660 Stewart Avenue.

660 is a co-op that can house up to 25 residents. This a roomy, relaxed house located just below west campus, with a spacious lawn and panoramic views of Cayuga Lake. Elected officers run the co-op and manage social activities. Members are responsible for house jobs and participation in meetings.

For more information, please email sixsixty@cornell.edu.
