Watermargin Cooperative

Watermargin is located on West Campus at 103 McGraw Place.

Watermargin is a co-op that can house up to 23 residents. Watermargin was founded by World War II veterans in order to promote a greater understanding of people of different race and religion. We still hold on to this ideal, and expand it all intergroup relationships. To increase diversity, we hold a random lottery in order to maximize diversity. We also encourage everyone to attend lectures, workshops, films and other events that celebrate diversity. All members are expected to contribute to cooking, cleaning and general upkeep.

For more information, please email watermargin@cornell.edu.
