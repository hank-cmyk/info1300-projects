Triphammer Cooperative

Triphammer Cooperative is located on North Campus at 150 Triphammer Road.

Triphammer Cooperative is a co-op that can house up to 20 residents, and holds many gatherings and activies such as pumpkin carving, camping, tie-dying, and bowling. There is also easy access to bus stops at this relaxed and friendly community. Residents have weekly chores, contributing to the general upkeep of the house.

For more information, please email triphammer@cornell.edu.
