Wait Avenue Cooperative

Wait Avenue is located on North Campus at 302 Wait Avenue.

At over 100 years old, this is one of the oldest buildings on North Campus. The Wait Avenue Cooperative provides a space for 14 women of varying stages in their academic career to act as a family. You can enjoy our spacious front porch, watching movies, and sharing meals with your fellow housemates. One to two officers manage daily operations, but all members must contribute to the maintenance of the house.

For more information, please email waitavenue@cornell.edu.
