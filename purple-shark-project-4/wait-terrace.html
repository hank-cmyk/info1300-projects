Wait Terrace Cooperative

Wait Terrace is located on North Campus at 308 Wait Avenue.

Wait Terrace is a co-op that can house up to 13 residents, who often enjoy socializing together, whether over meals or even, in good weather, on the house's porch, which contains a hammock, porch swing, and barbeque.

For more information, please email waitterrace@cornell.edu.
