Wari Cooperative

Wari Cooperative is located on North Campus at 208 Dearborn Place.

Wari Cooperative is a co-op that can house up to 10 residents. It is a close-knit community for women of color, who share in weekly house responsibilities. Residents can also be involved in many cultural and social activities that support women of color, and can help contribute to the spirit of unity and culture on the Cornell campus.

For more information, please email waricoop@cornell.edu.
